About us

ALFA ONE GEN. CON. EST. is fully proficient in handling all types of Mechanical and Steel Structural jobs, Pipe Lines, Civil Construction, Electrical & Instrumentation works, etc. Our business includes, but not restricted to Fabrication and erection of Pre-engineered Structural Buildings, Construction of Storage tanks, Chilling plants, Fabrication and Erection of Storage Shelters, Erection and Commissioning of Cooling Towers, Steel Buildings for Offices, Factories, Warehouse, etc., Fire rated Portable Offices, Skids, Hopers, Garbage collection containers, Ramps, Platforms, Fencing, Chimneys, Hoods, Power Panel Boards etc. We are fully capable of executing all type of shop fabrication works as well as project works including Pipe line, structural erection, shut downs, etc.

Our business practices are based on respect, ethics and responsibility. Steps are now being taken to establish new areas of business including industrial projects in JIZAN. With continued growth and expansion.

Our every employee believes value engineering is an important part of our work, and we provide solutions, not just products. Our teams of qualified, experienced engineers and technicians can create and build to client specifications, whatever the challenge. Our clients are ensured the highest levels of product quality and workmanship including on-time completion.
